Welcome to Speed ACM!

Speed ACM is the University of Louisville Student Chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery, sponsored by the Computer Science and Engineering department. We are the Registered Student Organization (RSO) dedicated to everything computing. The ACM is open to students and alumni of all majors and disciplines across campus. The one thing that all members have in common is an interest in technology.

Our lab is located in the basement of the J.B. Speed Building in room LL02. Members receive access to the lab via door code. The lab is a great place to hang out on campus; with plenty of table space, a large couch, TV, and a snack shop. We have tons of games, and resources for your education; including a printer, servers, textbooks, and even a 3D printer!

The ACM regularly hosts various types of events such as workshops and LAN parties. More experienced members organize Special Interest Groups (SIGs) and host weekly meetings regarding specific computing topics.
